Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share a household with my partner?
Create a household in the app, then share the invite code with your partner. They can join by tapping "Join with Invite Code" on the start screen.
Can I use WeSpend without creating an account?
Yes. Tap "Browse" on the login screen to explore all features with demo data. To sync across devices or share with a partner, you'll need to sign up.
How do I change the currency?
Your household's default currency is set when creating the household. You can record individual expenses in different currencies by long-pressing the currency icon on the expense entry screen.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Delete Account. All your personal data will be permanently removed.
Is my data secure?
Yes. WeSpend uses Firebase with TLS encryption for all data transmission and storage. We never share your financial data with third parties.
