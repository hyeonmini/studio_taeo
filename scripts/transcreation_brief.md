# WeSpend landing — transcreation brief

You are a **native marketing copywriter** for the locales assigned to you. Your job is
**transcreation, not translation**: write copy that feels like it was *written by a local*,
for a premium consumer app. Literal translations of the English below are NOT acceptable —
adapt idioms, rhythm, and even the *angle* of the message so it lands naturally in each culture.

## The product truth (never lose this)
**WeSpend** is an easy, shared budgeting app. Its signature magic:
- You pick a **photo or screenshot** of your spending (a bank app, a payment alert, a receipt
  photo) and the app **reads the transactions and sorts them into your budget automatically**.
- **No bank/card linking. No typing.**
- Card & account numbers are **masked on-device before analysis** (privacy).
- **Shared budgets** (partner / family / group) via a 6-digit invite, real-time sync.
- **Multi-currency**, near real-time rates; log in any currency, auto-converts.
- **Separate budgets per purpose** (travel / home / group); filter by category, payment, person.

## Tone
Warm, friendly, confident, a little witty. Premium but human. Short and punchy where marked.

## Creative freedom
The English version uses a "the easiest first step to getting **rich**" angle. **You do NOT have
to keep the "get rich" framing.** If a money/wealth angle feels crass, greedy, or off in your
culture, pick a locally resonant angle instead — e.g. *effortless control of your money*,
*peace of mind*, *spending that sorts itself*, *finally keeping a budget you stick to*,
*smart with money*. Keep it cohesive **within each locale** (hero + summit should rhyme
thematically). Be bold; this is a flagship marketing page.

## Hard rules
- Keep brand/product names verbatim: **WeSpend**, **App Store**, **Google Play**, **Studio Taeo**.
- Keep emojis where the English value has one (✦, 🔒). Plain text only — **no HTML, no `<br>`, no markdown**.
- Keep the numbers/quantities: **38** languages, **50+** currencies, **6-digit** invite, **16** colors, **5** at once, **~10 sec**, **0** typing, **7** (demo stamp).
- For the receipt block, **localize the currency** naturally (e.g. €, ¥, ₫, ₹, ₺, zł, ₽, R$ …) instead of `$`.
- Respect length: values must fit the same UI slot as English. Headline parts, chips, labels,
  and bullets must stay **short** (roughly the English length, ±a little).
